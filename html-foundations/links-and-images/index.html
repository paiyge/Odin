Absolute and Relative Links

Links to pages on other websites are absolute links. Comprised of the protocol, domain, and path, eg scheme://domain/path, or https://www.theodinproject.com/about

Links to pages within the same website are relative links.

Images

It is important to provide credit when using images from other sources.An easy way to provide credit is to include the creator’s name and contact info in a README file in your repository, or give attribution.

To find them - Google images and in image results -> Tools -> Usage rights -> “Creative Commons”. Choose & click on a image and click license details.
