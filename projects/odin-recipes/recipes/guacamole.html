Guacamole

Guacamole is a delicious and versatile Mexican dip made primarily from ripe avocados. It’s a perfect accompaniment to tortilla chips, tacos, burritos, or even as a spread for sandwiches. The creamy texture of the avocados combined with the fresh flavors of lime, cilantro, and onions creates a vibrant and flavorful dip that is loved by many.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocados with a fork until smooth or chunky, depending on your preference.
  3. Add the lime juice, red onion, jalapeño (if using), cilantro, and garlic to the bowl.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Mix everything together until well combined.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
